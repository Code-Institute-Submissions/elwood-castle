Frequently Asked Questions:

How will Covid-19 affect my visit?

Currently Elwood Castle has a clear social distancing policy.

Attendees to indoor events and tours are required to wear a mask, our reception has spares if you forget to bring your own. At all times you'll be required to keep 2 meters away from guests outside your bubble. If your booking has been affected by a lockdown you should have already been contacted, if not please Contact Us so we can move your booking to a new date.

What time do you open?

Elwood Castle is open to the public from 10:00am everyday. Last entry is at 16:00 unless you have tickets to an event starting after four.

Attendees without tickets to events occuring in the evening will have to leave the castle by 17:30.

Elwood Castle is shut Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.

Is the castle wheelchair accessible?

Elwood Castle is wheelchair accessible, along with every event offered.

Some areas of the castle are unaccessible to wheelchair users, but our guided tours do not enter these portions of the castle.

If I'm late to an event can I still attend?

Ticket holders are always permitted to join events after they start, although the event will end at the normal time.

Generally Elwood Castle cannot offer compensation for time lost for late attendees unless there are special circumstances. If you think you may be late please Contact Us before hand so we can do our best to accommodate for the delay.

How do I refund a booking?

All bookings are fully refundable up to three days before the date of the event.

Bookings are non-refundable if the date is less than three days before the event, unless there are special reasons for the cancellation. Please Contact Us if you have an issue relating to refunding a booking we'll try to resolve it for you.

Can I change the date of a booking?

We can change the event of a booking depending on how many tickets are avaliable for the new date.

Please Contact Us and we'll make sure to move your booking to a date that works for you.

I forgot my I.D. can I still attend an age restricted event?

Elwood Castle operates under Challenge 25 rules. If you are asked for your I.D. and cannot present it you will be asked to leave the event. Your ticket will not be refundable but we may be able to transfer your ticket to another day or book you on a different unrestricted event on the same day.

Do you offer parking?

Elwood Castle has ample car parking space for visitors, please only park in designated spots as you may damage areas of historical importance.
