Visiting Elwood Castle

Elwood Castle is a national heritage site and open to the public. Entry is free to the castle and grounds, Events such as guided tours, meals and shows can also be booked ahead of your visit.

The castle opens at 10:00am everyday, last entry (unless for pre-booked events) is at 16:00. For detailed information about our opening times please see our FAQ
